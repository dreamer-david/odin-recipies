Chicken Wings Recipe

Home

Description

These wings will be a mouth watering delight for anyone! Take a look at this quick and easy recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, prressed
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and Black pepper
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Combine the ingredients together in a sealable bag and shake them.
  3. Place the wings on a pan and cook in the oven for 30 to 45 minutes
