Mac and Cheese Recipe

Home

Description

This is a nice and very delightful mac and cheese recipe you can use whenever you're craving a nice meal.

Ingredients

  • Box of elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  • Bring a large pot of slightly salted water to a boil and leave the elbow macaroni inside for 8 minutes
  • Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat
  • Add flour,salt,and pepper and stir for 5 mintes
  • Add in milk slowly and stir continuosly for 5 minutes
  • Add Cheddar cheese and stir until melted for about 2 to 4 minutes
  • Drain the macaroni and fold into the cheese sauce until coated
