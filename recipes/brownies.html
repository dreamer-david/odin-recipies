Brownie Recipe

Home

Description

This recipe is a nice little brownie recipe that'll be sure to satsify a sweet tooth!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Water
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Oil
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Mix the dry and wet ingredients in seperate bowls
  2. Combine them together
  3. Pour in to a baking pan lined with parchment paper
  4. Bake in the oven at 325-degrees and for 40-45 minutes.
